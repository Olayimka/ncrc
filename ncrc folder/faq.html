Frequently Asked Questions

The Ideation
NCRC started as an initiative that emerged from an NYSC project in 2003. It began with a community development project in Damaturu, Yobe State, aimed at waste management and environmental education.
Coming up with the Name
The name "Nature Cares" was inspired by Genesis 2:15 in the Bible, highlighting man's responsibility to care for nature, which first cared for humans.
Step by Step Development
From 2003-2007, the initiative involved school programs, clean-up campaigns, and collaborations with local governments. It expanded to Lagos in 2008 and grew through partnerships and international training.
Early Days in Lagos
From 2008 onwards, Nature Cares engaged with the Lagos State Ministry of Environment and other partners, focusing on environmental education and capacity building.
Milestones Achieved
Over the years, NCRC gained international exposure, expanded its reach, registered as an NGO in 2015, and achieved strategic partnerships with numerous organizations.
