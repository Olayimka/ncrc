About NCRC

Nature Cares Resource Centre (NCRC) is a Knowledge Based NGO, bridging Environmental Education and Sustainability gaps via advocacy, research and capacity development. NCRC was founded July 2003 and fully registered as an (NGO) organization on the 29th day of April, 2015. Our certificate number is CAC/IT/NO 77383

Our Vision
To amplify the voice for Environmental Education (EE)

Our Mission
Promoting clean, safe and friendly environment.

Our Thematic Focus
Environment Education Capacity Building Advocacy Research and Training

For households and schools, we supply water treatment systems that remove 99.99% of bacteria from dirty water sources, eliminating widespread water borne diseases such as cholera, dysentery and diarrhea. This makes clean, safe drinking water continually available without the need to harvest local forests to boil water.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS IN
SUSTAINABLE PROJECTS

Livelihoods

Livelihood programs work to end extreme poverty, provide equitable education, and empower women and girls. They promote inclusive economic growth, productive employment, and decent work for all.

Successful projects

Successful projects focus on building resilient infrastructure, promoting sustainable industrialization, and fostering innovation. They also address climate change, promote inclusive societies, and strengthen accountable institutions.

Health

Health initiatives aim to promote well-being, manage water and sanitation, and improve nutrition through sustainable agriculture. They also focus on protecting ecosystems, managing forests, and combating desertification to ensure long-term health benefits.

Countries Benefited from the Program

Countries benefit by reducing inequality, ensuring access to sustainable energy, and creating inclusive, resilient cities. They also enhance global partnerships for sustainable development and strengthen implementation efforts.
